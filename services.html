How I Work

My Development Process

A straightforward process that keeps you informed and the work moving efficiently.

1

Understand the Brief

We discuss what you need — the purpose of the site, the audience, the pages, any specific design ideas or references. I ask questions until I have a clear picture.

2

Structure & Plan

I plan the page structure, decide on the layout approach (CSS Grid vs Flexbox), and map out the colour palette and typography before writing a single line of code.

3

Build & Refine

I build mobile-first, starting with the HTML structure, then CSS layout, then visual polish. I test at every breakpoint as I go — not just at the end.

4

Review & Deliver

You review the result. I take feedback seriously and make revisions until the site matches what was agreed. Then I hand over clean, organised files ready to deploy.
